How does LoveAndMatchmaking work?

LoveAndMatchmaking offers free and paid services. You can add your profile to the Love and Matchmaking database, which it calls a Lovebase, for free by filling out the company’s online form. Your profile remains confidential, but you will be eligible as a match for its clients.
With paid services from LoveAndMatchmaking, you receive personal coaching and local love introductions. Matchmakers give you one-on-one feedback on your dating profile and select matches for you based on research and its proprietary Human Enhanced Algorithm. Your matchmaker gets to know you through conversations and sticks with you to help with date coaching and early relationship building.
To sign up for LoveAndMatchmaking’s services, visit its website and fill out the interest form. Because LoveAndMatchmaking needs comprehensive information about you to determine your perfect match, the application form is fairly long.

Is LoveAndMatchmaking worth it?

LoveAndMatchmaking is individualized to every client and pairs you with a matchmaker who gets to know you and customizes your matches. The company offers a different kind of experience for those who find online dating impersonal or overwhelming. If you’re looking for a matchmaker who will also help you build a relationship, LoveAndMatchmaking could be right for you.

LoveAndMatchmaking cost

You can sign up for free with Love and Matchmaking. There is no cost to add your profile to the Lovebase the company uses for a portion of its matchmaking. 
This makes you available to be matched with its clients.The company also offers personalized services, matches and coaching. Call or fill out an online contact form to speak to someone about a quote.
